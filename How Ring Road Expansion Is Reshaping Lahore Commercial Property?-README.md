## Lahore Ring Road Expansion and Urban Growth

The Lahore Ring Road is a circular highway designed to connect all major entry and exit points of the city. Over time, its expansion has linked key roads such as Raiwind Road, Multan Road, Ferozepur Road, GT Road, and Canal Road. This improved connectivity has reduced travel time and opened new areas for residential and commercial development.

As accessibility improves, commercial activity naturally follows. Businesses prefer locations that are easy to reach for customers, suppliers, and employees. This is why areas connected to Ring Road are witnessing a surge in demand for commercial property for sale in lahore.
